MISSION:

It is the mission of GAFCCA to model and facilitate a nurturing environment for ourselves and our fellow family child care providers so that we may be empowered to deliver the best quality child care for the children and families we serve.


PURPOSE:

1. To promote the development of professionalism within the family child care environment as well as personal growth of the membership.

2. To assist members in achieving these goals through support, mentoring, training and fellowship.

3. To advance the cause of family child care providers within our homes and the communities in which we live.

BENEFITS:
